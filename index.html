What is Konfam?

Konfam is a Web3 product authenticator built
on the blockchain. It's a blockchain database
hosting approved products: pharmaceuticals, health
and consumables. Consumers can easily konfam if a
product is genuine or harmful.

How to Konfam?

Enter the batch number of the product
in the interface labelled Enter batch no. and click check.
